The Luxor Temple was built during the New Kingdom and dedicated to the Theban Triad consisted of Amun,
his consort Mut,and their son Khonsu. The focus of the annual Opet Festival,Its construction was begun
by Pharaoh Amenhotep III and was completed by Pharaoh Tutankhamun.

From the Middle Ages,the population of Luxor had settled in and around the temple,at the southward end of the mount.
Due to this, centuries of rubble had accumulated, to the point where there was an artificial hill some 14.5 to 15 metres in height.
