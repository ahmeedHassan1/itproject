In ancient Egyptian history, dynasties are series of rulers sharing a common origin.
They are usually, but not always, traditionally divided into 33 pharaonic dynasties.
Egypt's most famous pharaohs were from the Eighteenth Dynasty, including
Tutankhamun,Hatshepsut,Rmeses ll and Nefertiti.
